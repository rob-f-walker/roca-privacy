Privacy Policy for ROCA: Two-Player Strategy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

ROCA is a local two-player strategy game. We respect your privacy and have designed the app to collect as little information as possible.

What we collect: Nothing. ROCA stores your game settings (display names, color palette, board size, mode) and your in-progress and completed games on your device only, using the operating system's standard storage. None of this leaves your device.

What we share: Nothing. We do not transmit, sell, or share any data with third parties.

Analytics & tracking: None. ROCA does not contain analytics, advertising, or tracking code of any kind.

Network use: None in this version.

Children: ROCA is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Contact: For questions about this policy, email rocasupport@icloud.com
